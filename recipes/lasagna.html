Lasagna


Hearty and delicious, a firm family favourite.

Make a big batch and freeze some to enjoy another day.

Ingredients

  • 500g of beef mince
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jar of pasatta
  • 1 tin of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 6 lasagna sheets
  • 200g of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 200g of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the beef mince
  2. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, pepper, and parsley
  4. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg
  5. Layer noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella, and meat sauce in a baking dish
  6. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan cheese
  7. Bake for 45 minutes at 375Â°F
