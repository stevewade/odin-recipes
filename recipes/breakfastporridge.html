Breakfast Porridge


Kickstart your day with a healthy breakfast

The slow release of energy will keep you fuelled until lunch.

Ingredients

  • 75g of oats
  • 150ml of milk
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of mixed seeds
    (pumpkin, sunflower, chia etc.)

Instructions

  1. Combine oats and milk in a pan
  2. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes,
    stirring occasionally
  3. Remove from heat and let sit for 2 minutes
  4. Top with banana, honey, and seeds
