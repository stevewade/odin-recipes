Beans on Toast


A British Mainstay and Ultimate Comfort Food

Universally loved, the key to its appeal is its simplicity.

Add cheese if you're feeling extra.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of baked beans
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Heat the beans in a pan
  3. Spread butter on the toast
  4. Pour the beans on top
  5. Add cheese if desired
