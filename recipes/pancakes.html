Pancakes


Delicious, light and fluffy

How high will you stack them?

Ingredients

  • 225g of flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 350ml milk
  • 50g melted butter

Instructions

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, and butter in a separate bowl
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients
  4. Stir until just combined
  5. Heat a pan over medium heat
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter into the pan
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface
  8. Flip and cook for another minute
